Apply to Rent

Ready to make a Madsen Properties home yours? Review the requirements below and submit your application online.

Application Requirements

All applications are processed in the order received. To be considered for a rental, each applicant must meet our standard qualification criteria. Please review the requirements carefully before applying.

  • All occupants 18 or older must apply separately. Each adult who will reside in the unit must submit their own application and pay the application fee.
  • $40 non-refundable application fee per applicant, paid at time of submission.
  • Credit score of 650 or higher. All applicants must demonstrate well-established credit history meeting this minimum threshold.
  • Verifiable income. Combined household income should typically be at least 2.5–3× the monthly rent. Proof of income (pay stubs, bank statements, offer letter) will be required.
  • Rental history verification. We contact previous landlords to verify your payment history and tenancy in good standing.
  • Background check. A standard background screening is included as part of the application process.

How to Apply

Click the "Apply Now" button to access our secure online application portal. Have your ID, proof of income, and payment method ready. Once submitted, our team will review your application typically within 1–3 business days.

If you have questions about a specific property or want to schedule a showing before applying, contact us at (530) 570-6192 or team@madsenpropertymgmt.com.

⚠️ Note: Submitting an application does not guarantee approval or reserve a unit. Properties remain available until a lease is fully executed. We recommend viewing the property and confirming availability before applying.
