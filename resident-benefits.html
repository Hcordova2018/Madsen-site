Insurance Details

Liability Insurance Requirements & Options

The Landlord requires all tenants to obtain liability coverage of at least $100,000 in property damage and personal liability from an A-rated carrier, maintained throughout the entire lease term. You have two options to satisfy this requirement:

Option 1 — Master Policy (Automatic Enrollment)

If you do not provide evidence of required insurance coverage by the lease commencement date, you will be automatically enrolled into the Landlord's Master Policy as part of the RBP. Coverage begins on your lease effective date and continues for the full term. The monthly premium is $11.95, included in your RBP fee.

Option 2 — Tenant's Own Policy

You may find, purchase, and maintain your own policy that meets the landlord's requirements. You must provide evidence of coverage by the lease commencement date. Your policy must meet all of the following criteria:

  • Purchased from an A- rated carrier
  • Meets or exceeds $100,000 in property damage and personal liability
  • Madsen Properties is listed as additional interest
  • Madsen Properties address listed as: PO Box 660121, Dallas, TX 75266

To submit proof of your own insurance, visit: insurance.residentforms.com

Important Notes

It is the tenant's sole responsibility to timely pay premiums directly to their insurance provider to avoid cancellation of coverage. If a tenant's policy lapses or is cancelled, the tenant will be automatically enrolled into the Master Policy and charged the applicable monthly premium.

Your choice of insurance option shall not affect whether your lease application is approved or the terms of your lease agreement.
