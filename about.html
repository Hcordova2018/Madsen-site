The Madsen Properties Team

A dedicated group of professionals committed to making property management seamless for owners and residents alike.

Alicia Madsen
Owner & Licensed Broker — CalBRE #01912310
Alicia graduated in 2005 from CSU, Chico with a BS in Business Administration, minor in Agricultural Business with an option in marketing. After purchasing her first investment property in 2009, she fell in love with real estate.

She has years of experience managing single family residences, apartments, town homes and duplexes in Butte County. In her free time, Alicia loves spending time outdoors, gardening, hiking, kayaking, and fishing with her husband and daughters.
