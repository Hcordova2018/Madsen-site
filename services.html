Our Services

Full-service property management for owners and investors across Chico, Paradise, Oroville & Butte County.

Professional Management, Tailored to You

At Madsen Properties, we handle every aspect of property ownership so you don't have to. From marketing your vacant unit to managing day-to-day tenant relations, our experienced team provides hands-on, responsive management that protects your investment and keeps your tenants happy.

We manage single-family homes, condominiums, townhouses, and small multi-unit properties throughout Butte County. Whether you're a first-time landlord or a seasoned investor, we have the expertise to maximize your rental income while minimizing stress.

Tenant Screening

We conduct thorough background checks, credit checks (650+ score required), rental history verification, and income verification to place only qualified, reliable tenants in your property.

Property Marketing

Your vacancy gets maximum exposure through listings on Zillow, Trulia, HotPads, Craigslist, and more — bringing in a large, qualified applicant pool to minimize your vacancy time.

Rent Collection

Tenants pay online with one-time or recurring payment options. We handle all rent collection, late notices, and disbursements — you receive your money on time, every time.

Maintenance & Repairs

Tenants can submit maintenance requests 24/7 through our online portal. We coordinate with our trusted network of vendors to ensure fast, cost-effective repairs that protect your property.

Lease Management

We prepare legally compliant California lease agreements, handle renewals, enforce lease terms, and manage the move-in and move-out process including security deposit accounting.

Monthly Accounting

Receive detailed monthly financial statements covering income, expenses, and disbursements. We provide complete transparency so you always know exactly how your investment is performing.

After-Hours Availability

Property emergencies don't keep business hours. We offer after-hours and weekend availability to handle urgent maintenance issues and tenant concerns when they arise.

Tenant Relations

Retaining quality tenants is our top priority. We foster positive landlord-tenant relationships, handle communications professionally, and work to keep your best tenants in place long-term.

Resident Benefits Package

Our optional RBP ($35–$40/mo) gives tenants credit building, identity protection, renters insurance, a rewards program, and move-in concierge — keeping great tenants happier, longer.
